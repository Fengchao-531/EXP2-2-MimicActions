✨ Exclusive Share: A super easy way to grab flight discount codes (perfect for frequent travelers & students) — I tried it myself and it worked! 💙

I booked Melbourne → Sydney and saved about AUD 50. Here’s the full flow so you can try too:

1️⃣ Open the activity page: Visit Contact organizer (webmail compose) and fill in the form. Remember: enter the correct flight number, because each airline has its own promo codes.

2️⃣ Fill in required fields: Name, email, etc. (The promo code will be sent to your email, so make sure it’s correct!)

3️⃣ Receive your Promo Code: Check your inbox for something like XXXX-YYYY. Copy & paste it at checkout when booking.

4️⃣ See your results: The page will also return a table with all your promo codes and their status.

💡 Extra tip for group trips: Upload a file with everyone’s info, and the system will generate a group promo code automatically — no need to enter each person’s details one by one. Super convenient!
🔹 Buttons might say “Claim” or “Submit Application” — just follow the prompt.
🔹 For multi-trip or group bookings, uploading a file is way easier.
